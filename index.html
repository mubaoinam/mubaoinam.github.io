Hello! I am Oinam, welcome to my webpage!

I am currently a postdoc in the Mayhall group at Virginia Tech. I work on method development for molecular simulations using clasical algorithms on one hand and quantum algorithms on the other.

I did my Ph.D. from University of Erlangen, Germany. I got my Master's degree from VNIT Nagpur, India and Bachelor's degree from MCC Chennai, India. I was a summer research fellow at NCL Pune, India.
