Hello! I am Oinam, welcome to my webpage!

I am currently a Postdoc at Virginia Tech. My work focuses on (a) development of quantum algorithms for molecular simulations on near-term quantum computers, and (b) method development, implementation and application for electronic structure theory.

I did my Ph.D. from Friedrich-Alexander University Erlangen, Germany. I have a Master's degree in Chemistry from Visvesvaraya National Institute of Technology Nagpur, India and a Bachelor's degree in Chemistry from Madras Christian College, India. I was a summer research fellow at NCL Pune, India.
